  • Deceptive Links: "paypal.com-login-account-verify.com" is NOT PayPal.

  • Generic Greetings: "Dear Customer" instead of your name often signals bulk spam.
  • Attachment Traps: Unexpected invoices or shipping details ZIP files contain malware.
